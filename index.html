Experience

Throughout the course of my four-year degree, I have been involved in student governance in some way each year. Currently, I am the Student Governance Officer. My job is to advise and create resources for student representatives, including those on Faculty Associations, General Faculties Council, and Council. Last year, I was a student councillor for the Faculty of Arts. As a councillor I sat on Policy Committee and Elections Review Committee. Also in my third year I was on Academic Relations Group, which is advisory to the Vice- President Academic. For this committee I proxied for the Vice- President Academic on the Undergraduate Writing Committee and the Undergraduate Research Selection Committee. In my second year, I was the Events Coordinator for the Collective Body for Arts Students, which is the Faculty Association for the Faculty of Arts. In my first year, I was a student representative on Arts Faculty Committee and Arts Executive Committee, which are the top governance bodies for the Faculty of Arts. My experiences in all of these positions were amazing and I believe that each of the skills I gained and things I learned are directly applicable to being VP Academic.
